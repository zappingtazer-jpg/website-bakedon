Blueberry Cheesecake Brioche Buns

Soft, golden brioche buns filled with creamy cheesecake and sweet blueberry jam — a perfect blend of rich, fluffy, and fruity indulgence.

Prep Time: 25 mins

Rise Time: 1 hr 30 mins

Bake Time: 20 mins

Servings: 8 buns

Ingredients

For the brioche dough:

  • 2¾ cups (345g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 packet (7g) instant yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup (120ml) warm milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup (75g) unsalted butter, softened

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 200g cream cheese, softened
  • 3 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the blueberry topping:

  • ½ cup (120g) blueberry jam or preserves
  • Fresh blueberries (optional, for garnish)

For brushing:

  • 1 egg yolk + 1 tbsp milk (egg wash)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add warm milk and eggs, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead in the softened butter, one tablespoon at a time, until smooth and elastic (8–10 minutes).
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1–1½ hours, until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and divide into 8 equal balls. Flatten each slightly and place on a lined baking tray.
  6. Press a deep well in the center of each bun using your fingers or the bottom of a glass.
  7. Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Spoon 1 tbsp into each bun center.
  8. Top with 1 tsp of blueberry jam.
  9. Brush the edges with egg wash and let rest for 15 minutes while preheating oven to 180°C (350°F).
  10. Bake for 18–20 minutes or until golden brown.
  11. Cool slightly before serving. Garnish with fresh blueberries if desired.
