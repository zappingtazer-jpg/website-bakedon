Apple Crumble

Apple crumble combines tender, spiced apples with a golden buttery topping that’s crisp on the outside and soft within — the ultimate cozy dessert.

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Servings: 6

Ingredients

For the filling:

  • 6 medium apples (about 800g), peeled and sliced
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour

For the crumble topping:

  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • ½ cup (50g) rolled oats
  • ¼ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the apple slices with brown sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, and flour. Mix until coated.
  3. Pour the apple mixture into a baking dish (about 8x8 inches).
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, oats, and salt. Cut in the butter using your fingers or a fork until the mixture forms crumbs.
  5. Sprinkle the crumble mixture evenly over the apples.
  6. Bake for 35–40 minutes or until the topping is golden and the apples are bubbling.
  7. Allow to cool slightly before serving. Serve warm with ice cream or custard.
