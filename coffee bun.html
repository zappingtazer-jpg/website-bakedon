Coffee Bun

A soft, buttery bun with a crisp coffee-flavored topping — perfectly golden and fragrant, best enjoyed warm with a cup of coffee or tea.

Prep Time: 30 mins

Bake Time: 20 mins

Servings: 6 buns

Ingredients

For the Bread Dough:

  • 250g bread flour
  • 30g sugar
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 120ml warm milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 30g unsalted butter (softened)

For the Butter Filling:

  • 60g unsalted butter (chilled and cubed)

For the Coffee Topping:

  • 50g unsalted butter (softened)
  • 40g powdered sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp instant coffee (dissolved in 1 tbsp hot water)
  • 50g all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix bread flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add warm milk and egg, then knead until a dough forms.
  2. Add softened butter and knead until smooth and elastic (about 10 minutes). Cover and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  3. Shape the Buns: Divide the dough into 6 portions. Flatten each piece, place a cube of chilled butter in the center, and seal tightly into a ball. Arrange on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  4. Cover and let rise again for 30–40 minutes, until puffy.
  5. Make the Coffee Topping: Cream butter and sugar until light. Add egg and coffee mixture, then mix in flour until smooth. Transfer to a piping bag.
  6. Pipe a spiral layer of the coffee topping over each bun, leaving a small gap at the bottom.
  7. Bake: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake for 15–20 minutes, until golden brown and fragrant.
  8. Let cool slightly and serve warm — the coffee crust should be crisp, with a soft buttery center inside.
