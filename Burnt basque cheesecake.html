Burnt Basque Cheesecake

Creamy, crustless, and caramelized — this Burnt Basque Cheesecake features a rich, custardy center with a deep brown top for irresistible flavor and texture.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Bake Time: 45 mins

Chill Time: 4 hrs

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 500g cream cheese, room temperature
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1½ cups (360ml) heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 210°C (410°F). Line a 9-inch (23cm) springform pan with parchment paper, ensuring it comes up the sides.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  5. Sift in the flour and salt, then gently fold to combine — don’t overmix.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and tap lightly to remove air bubbles.
  7. Bake for 45–50 minutes, until deeply golden brown on top and slightly jiggly in the center.
  8. Let cool completely in the pan, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
  9. Slice and serve chilled or at room temperature.
