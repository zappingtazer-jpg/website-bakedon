Blueberry Cinnamon Rolls

Soft, buttery cinnamon rolls swirled with juicy blueberries and topped with a luscious cream cheese glaze — the ultimate cozy breakfast treat.

Prep Time: 25 mins

Rise Time: 1 hr 30 mins

Bake Time: 25 mins

Servings: 10 rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 2¼ tsp (7g) instant yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm milk
  • ¼ cup (60g) melted butter
  • 1 large egg

For the filling:

  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup (150g) fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1 tbsp flour (to toss with blueberries)

For the cream cheese glaze:

  • 100g cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tbsp butter, softened
  • ½ cup (60g) powdered sugar
  • 1–2 tbsp milk
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add warm milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. In a small bowl, mix brown sugar and cinnamon for the filling.
  5. Punch down dough and roll it out into a large rectangle (about 12x16 inches).
  6. Brush with melted butter, sprinkle the cinnamon sugar, and scatter floured blueberries evenly on top.
  7. Roll up tightly from the long side, then slice into 10 rolls.
  8. Arrange in a greased baking dish. Cover and let rise again for 30–40 minutes.
  9. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes, until golden brown.
  10. Meanwhile, beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth.
  11. Spread glaze over warm rolls and enjoy!
