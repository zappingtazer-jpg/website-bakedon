Dessert

Klappertart

A creamy Indonesian coconut custard dessert with soft young coconut, warm spices, and a golden baked topping — a true classic from Manado.

Prep Time: 25 mins

Bake Time: 30 mins

Servings: 6

Ingredients

Custard Base:

  • 500 ml milk
  • 100 g sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 30 g cornstarch
  • 25 g butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Main Filling:

  • 100 g young coconut flesh (thinly sliced)
  • 30 g raisins (optional)
  • 20 g chopped almonds or cashews (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Custard: In a saucepan, heat milk over medium heat. In a bowl, whisk together sugar, egg yolks, cornstarch, and salt until smooth.
  2. Gradually pour the warm milk into the egg mixture while stirring continuously to temper it. Return to the pan and cook until thickened and creamy.
  3. Once thick, add butter, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Stir well until smooth.
  4. Add the Coconut: Mix in young coconut flesh and raisins. Pour the custard mixture into ramekins or a baking dish.
  5. Prepare Meringue (optional): Whisk egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks form. Spread or pipe over the custard surface.
  6. Bake: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake for about 25–30 minutes, until the top is golden and slightly firm.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle with chopped almonds or cashews.
  8. Serve warm or chilled — the creamy custard and coconut blend perfectly with the hint of spice.
