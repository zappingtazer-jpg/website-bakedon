Brown Butter Brookies

A perfect blend of fudgy brownies and chewy cookies — brown butter adds a rich, nutty depth that makes these brookies irresistible.

Prep Time: 25 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Servings: 12 squares

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, browned
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • Brownie batter of your choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Line a square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Brown the butter in a saucepan until golden and nutty. Let it cool slightly.
  3. Whisk together the sugars and browned butter. Add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Fold in dry ingredients, then stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Spread half the cookie dough in the pan, pour brownie batter on top, and finish with remaining dough.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until set. Cool before cutting into squares.
