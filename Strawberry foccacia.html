Strawberry Focaccia

A sweet twist on classic focaccia — soft, olive-oil-rich bread topped with juicy strawberries, honey, and a sprinkle of sea salt. Perfectly chewy, fragrant, and golden.

Prep Time: 20 mins + 1 hr rising

Bake Time: 25 mins

Servings: 6–8

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (7g) instant yeast
  • 1 cup (240ml) warm water
  • 2 1/2 cups (320g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (plus extra for drizzling)

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sugar or honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt flakes
  • Optional: a few basil leaves or rosemary sprigs

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and honey. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a shaggy dough forms, then knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Coat a bowl lightly with olive oil, place the dough inside, cover, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Shape the Focaccia: Grease a baking pan (about 9x13 inches) with olive oil. Press the dough evenly into the pan, dimple it with your fingertips, and drizzle with more olive oil.
  5. Arrange strawberry slices on top, drizzle lightly with honey or sugar, and sprinkle with sea salt. Add herbs if desired.
  6. Bake: Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Bake for 20–25 minutes, until golden brown and crisp at the edges.
  7. Cool slightly before slicing. Serve warm — delicious on its own or with whipped ricotta or cream cheese.
